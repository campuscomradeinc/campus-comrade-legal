👥

Friends only

Your status is only visible to people you've mutually added as friends. No strangers can see your information.

📍

No location data

Campus Comrade never accesses your GPS or location. You manually set your status — you're always in control.

⚡

Live updates

When a friend updates their status, your home screen updates automatically. No refreshing needed.

🔔

Push notifications

Get notified the moment a friend arrives on campus so you can plan to meet up between classes.

🏛️

Building & activity

See exactly where your friends are and what they're up to — studying, classes, clubs, lectures and more.

🔍

Find by username

Add friends by searching their username. Send a request and once accepted they appear on your feed instantly.

Up and running in minutes

1

Create an account

Sign up with your university email and choose a username.

2

Add your friends

Search for friends by username and send them a request.

3

Update your status

Tap Status, select On campus, pick your building and activity.

4

See who's around

Your home screen shows exactly who's on campus and what they're doing.

🔒

Your privacy is our priority

No location tracking. No ads. No selling your data. Campus Comrade collects only what's needed to run the app and nothing more. Read our full privacy policy.
